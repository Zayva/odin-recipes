Mini Quiche

Description

This is a recipe for mini quiche.

Ingredients

  • 1 Brown Onion diced
  • 600g of Bacon diced
  • 2 cups of Shreaded cheese
  • 10 Extra large eggs
  • 1 cup of Milk
  • 4 sheets of Puff pastry
  • 1 teaspoon of Paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of Garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of Ground pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Mix onion, bacon, cheese, and spices in a bowl.
  2. Whisk eggs in a separate bowl, then stir in milk.
  3. Cut pastry into thirds horizontally and vertically, so that there are 9 squares from each sheet.
  4. Preheat oven to 180C.
  5. Press pastry squares into silicon cupcake moulds, then spoon in fillings and pour over egg mixture.
  6. Crack pepper on top, and put into oven for 20 minutes.
